About US

In Merge Witches, players embark on a magical journey where they assume the role of a witch tasked with restoring her realm. The game involves merging different items, such as magical ingredients, to create powerful spells and items. As players progress through various levels, they solve challenging puzzles to unlock new areas, collect resources, and discover hidden treasures.
